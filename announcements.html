Announcements

The ECU Chancellor Search Committee will hold a number of listening sessions throughout the month of January 2020 in conjunction with the search. The purpose of these sessions is to get stakeholder feedback and input regarding the desired characteristics, skills, experience, and other capabilities of the next Chancellor. A list of scheduled listening sessions/forums are below. There may be additional sessions added at a future date, check this page for the most up-to-date list.
