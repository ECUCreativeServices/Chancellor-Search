Leadership Profile

The Board of Trustees and campus community of East Carolina University seek a servant leader to advance the institution for the 21st century and beyond, who is committed to ECU’s mission of student success, public service and regional transformation. We seek a leader who will embody ECU’s motto, Servire: “To Serve.”
