Athletics and Facilities

Pirate athletics are the pride of the Pirate Nation™. Arrrgh™! With 20 Division I teams, ECU is committed to the principle of institutional control in operating its athletics programs. This task is accomplished by operating in accordance with NCAA, the American Athletic Conference and university rules and regulations. As of fall 2019:
  • 59% of student-athletes earned academic honors
  • 3.16 average GPA for our full-time undergraduate student-athletes
  • 86% six-year graduation rate for student-athletes
